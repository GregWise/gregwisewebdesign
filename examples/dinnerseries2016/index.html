Ad Lounge invites graduating students of Advertising, Marketing, Communications, Public Relations, Business, Graphic Design, Web Development and Digital programs to a free evening of networking over dinner & drinks with leading industry executives. You apply. We select the top applicants. You attend an exciting training session. The rest is up to you.

Students

Are you an ambitious student about to cross the bridge from academia to that much talked-of and incredibly mysterious "real world"? Rest easy, my talented friend, you've stumbled upon the right page.

We'd like to offer you not only the training to develop your personal brand, but also an opportunity to dazzle the industry's greatest over a fancy dinner, as you literally rub elbows with its top leaders and your esteemed peers.

Now you might be wondering, "How on earth could I afford all this?" Fear not. We were students once! Everything is… wait for it… free! We'll give you a minute to pick your jaw up off the ground. Believe us, it's as awesome as it sounds.

Here's what's in it for you

Learn the art of preparing for and nailing an interview.

Receive constructive feedback on your resume and/or portfolio (if applicable).

Receive world-class training from our partner, Combustion Creative.

Meet your potential boss or mentor over free dinner and drinks. How awesome would what story be?

Bragging rights on your resume. Hiring managers now look for the “Next Gen Dinner Series Finalist” stamp on resumes. True story. We know because we talk to them every day.

Requirements

You're a graduating student from a marketing, advertising, communications, design or digital media program.

You can prove that you stand out among your peers in some way.

You're passionate about the industry you wish to work in, and can show us how.

What's so great about the Next Gen Dinner Series? That's for you to decide. Don't stall, your future awaits!
